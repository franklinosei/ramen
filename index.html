Best Ramen Joint

Ingredients:

  • 4 packs of ramen noodles
  • 8 cups of chicken or pork broth
  • 1/2 lb. pork belly
  • 1/2 lb. shiitake mushrooms
  • 4 soft boiled eggs
  • 2 cups of sliced green onions
  • 1 cup of bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. of miso paste
  • 2 tbsp. of mirin
  • 1 tbsp. of sesame oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. of grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken or pork broth to a simmer. Add the garlic, ginger, soy sauce, miso paste, mirin, and sesame oil, and stir well. Let the broth simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the flavors have melded together.
  2. While the broth is simmering, cook the pork belly in a separate pan over medium heat. Cook until crispy, then remove from the pan and slice into thin pieces.
  3. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms to the broth, and simmer for another 5-10 minutes until they are soft and tender.
  4. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  5. To assemble the ramen bowls, divide the cooked noodles into four large bowls. Ladle the hot broth and mushrooms over the noodles, then top each bowl with sliced pork belly, a soft boiled egg, green onions, and bean sprouts.
  6. Serve the ramen hot, and enjoy!
